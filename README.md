# Confidence Receipts Engine

**Idea:** A "Competence & Confidence" aggregator that automatically pulls "evidence of capability" from Kian's workspace, logs, and manual entries to build an undeniable case for self-trust.

## Why this matters
Kian identified **Confidence & self-confidence** as his #1 development priority (2026-02-25). 
The "Confidence Receipts Engine" combats the "Compounding Killer" (making/unmaking cycle) by providing objective, historical evidence of wins, completions, and smart decisions. When the urge to restart hits, this engine shows the receipts of what has already been built and why the current judgment is trustworthy.

## Core Features
1. **The Receipt Log (`receipts.json`)**: A structured log of "competence receipts" (Wins, Completions, Fast Decisions, Smart Moves).
2. **Evidence Collector**: Simple CLI and Web UI to log receipts with "Confidence Level" and "Impact".
3. **Receipt Wall**: A visual, high-impact dashboard displaying the cumulative evidence of capability.
4. **Streak Tracking**: Tracks the "Commitment Follow-Through" streak.

## How to use
- `python engine.py add`: Log a new receipt.
- `python engine.py summary`: Get a punchy summary of your current competence receipts.
- Open `index.html` to view the Receipt Wall.
